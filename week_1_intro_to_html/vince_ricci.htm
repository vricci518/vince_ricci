About Me:

Hello, my name is Vince and I'm a highly skilled Graphic Artist with a strong background in design,illustration, and layouts. I have a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Photography from SUNY Buffalo State College.
In my spare time I'm involved with charity fundraising with the Imperial Court of Buffalo and I currently sit on the Board of Directors as President. I'm also an avid knitter, having first learned how to knit in the late 1980's. I'm an active member of the Knitting Guild of Greater Buffalo as well as the Men Who Knit community. I've attended several Men's Knitting Retreats and have recently started producing a Video Podcast with my knitting BBF. We are known as Boozie Bears Knitting Podcast.

I am going to be a Front End Developer!

I've recently enrolled in a Continuing Education course in Web Development to expand my knowledge base and open myself to opportunities in web design.
My steps to get there:

  1. Register for the online course through Workforce Buffalo.
  2. Attend weekly classes.
  3. Participate in the group sessions during labs.
  4. Build a personal portfolio web site highighting my skills.
  5. Receive MTA Certification.
